Script Types Numbers (:Type) are as follows - 

                        0 - Abjad   1 - Alphabet  2 - Abugida   3 - Syllabery

                        4 - Ideogram-Logogram-Pictogram         5 - Braille  

                        6 - Sign    7 - Code-Shorthand-Emojis   8 - Unclassified
